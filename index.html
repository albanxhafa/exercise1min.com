    How it works

    The smallest useful loop.

    1. 01

      A reminder with bite

      Choose every 1, 2, or 3 hours and your active window. A savage roast arrives locally; start, snooze, or pause the rest of the day.

    2. 02

      A routine that fits

      Open the exact routine you previewed. Follow each illustrated move at your pace, replace one if needed, and never race a countdown.

    3. 03

      Back to your day

      Finish and log one honest movement break. Your breaks, minutes, and gentle streak grow—without calories, leaderboards, or guilt.

    What you get

    Thoughtful defaults. Full control.

    Local reminder schedule

    Your 1, 2, or 3-hour rhythm runs on-device. Permission and schedule health are shown clearly—never silently assumed.

    Illustrated exercises

    48 quick movements, each with an original animated figure, a clear guide, benefits, and body-area labels.

    Quiet progress

    FAQ

    Clear answers, no fine print.

    The app is still in development. Its core behavior and privacy model are already defined.

    What is Exercise 1 Min?

    Exercise 1 Min is a coming-soon mobile app for short movement breaks. It builds a desk-friendly routine, guides each move without a countdown, and gets you back to your day.

    Do I need equipment or a gym?

    No. Routines use bodyweight movements you can do at home or work. Floor exercises are optional and off by default, so the standard routine is easy to do beside a desk.

    How often should I take an exercise break?

    You choose the rhythm. The app can remind you every 1, 2, or 3 hours during the hours you select, and you can snooze or skip any reminder.

    Is Exercise 1 Min private?

    Yes. The mobile app works fully offline with no accounts, analytics, sign-up, or network calls. Your settings and exercise history stay on your device.

    Is the minute a countdown?

    No. “One minute” is a promise that the break is quick, not a timer you have to beat. You move through the illustrated routine with previous and next controls at your own pace.

    Is Exercise 1 Min free?

    The core app is planned to be free, with no ads, subscriptions, or upsell during the moment you are meant to move.

    When will the app be available?

    Exercise 1 Min is coming soon for iOS and Android. This page will be updated when the app is ready to download.
