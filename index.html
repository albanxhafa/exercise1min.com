You don't need a gym.
You need a minute.

Most of us sit for hours — coding, studying, in meetings. The fix isn't a workout plan you'll quit. It's one small movement, often enough to matter.

How it works

  1. 01

    A gentle reminder

    Every 1, 2, or 3 hours within the hours you choose, a calm notification nudges you to move. Snooze or skip freely — missing one is never a failure.

  2. 02

    One minute of moves

    Tap start and get a handful of quick, illustrated moves. No ticking timer — just do them at your own pace and tap Done.

  3. 03

    Back to your day

    That's it. A little movement banked, a quiet streak growing. No calories, no leaderboards, no pressure.
